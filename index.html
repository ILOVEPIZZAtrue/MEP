MEP TRANSYLVANIA
What is MEP?
MEP (Model European Parliament) is a program designed to increase students' interest and skills in debating international issues. It follows the debate model of the European Parliament, allowing participants to discuss various topics. The event aims to educate young people about European policies and enhance their awareness of European institutions. It will take place in Cluj-Napoca, Romania.
Who can participate in MEP?
High school students aged 14-19 can participate in the MEP. The event seeks individuals passionate about politics, as well as students eager to learn new information or develop their debating and communication skills. It encourages participation from students of all backgrounds, both from rural and urban areas.
How much does it cost?
The event will be funded through financial aid and scholarships to ensure that all participants, regardless of their financial background, can attend without any problems.
